Intestinal organoid TEER monolayer assay
Validated ✓
01

Dissociate organoids with TrypLE Express for 5 min at 37°C; pipette to single cells, filter through 70 µm strainer.

02

Seed at 2×10⁵ cells/cm² on Matrigel-coated 24-well transwell inserts (0.4 µm pore) in expansion medium.

03

Monitor TEER daily with EVOM3. Switch to differentiation medium at day 3–4 when TEER plateau is first detected.

04

Stable monolayer expected by day 6–8. Add DAPT (10 µM) to promote absorptive differentiation if needed.
